Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shalmi Online?
Shalmi Online is a platform where buyers and sellers connect to exchange a variety of products. We provide secure and reliable e-commerce solutions.
How do I place an order?
You can place an order by browsing products, adding items to your cart, and checking out through our secure payment system.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as popular digital wallets like PayPal and Stripe.
How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Please contact our customer service for assistance with returns.
