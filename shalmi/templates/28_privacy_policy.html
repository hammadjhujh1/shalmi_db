Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Shalmi Online, we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you create an account, make a purchase, or interact with our services. We also collect non-personal data like browsing patterns and device information.

Data Usage

Your personal data is used to facilitate your use of our platform, process orders, improve services, and communicate with you. We may also use aggregated data for analytics to enhance the overall user experience.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Your information is stored securely, and we regularly review our data protection protocols to ensure compliance with industry standards.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in cases where it is necessary to fulfill your order (e.g., sharing delivery details with couriers) or as required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and providing relevant content. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our support team.
