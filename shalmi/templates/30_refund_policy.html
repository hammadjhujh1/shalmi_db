Refund Policy

Overview

At Shalmi Online, we strive to ensure that our customers are satisfied with their purchases. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which refunds will be granted and the process for requesting a refund.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, your request must be made within 30 days of the purchase date. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. Refunds are not applicable to digital products or items marked as final sale.

Return Process

If your item qualifies for a return, please contact our customer support team to initiate the return process. Once your return is approved, we will provide you with the shipping details. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the product is defective or incorrect.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days, and the amount will be credited back to your original method of payment.

Exceptions

Certain products, such as perishable goods, custom-made items, and downloadable software, are not eligible for refunds. Please review the product description carefully before making your purchase.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance with your return, please feel free to contact our customer support team at support@shalmi.com.
